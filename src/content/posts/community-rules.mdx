# Main rules

These rules apply to all altie122 communities, including Discord, Twitch, YouTube, TikTok, and more.

## 1. Be respectful

No hate, harassment, or unnecessary drama is tolerated in this community, regardless of your opinions. This means absolutely no racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, etc. Treat others with the respect you would like to be treated with.

## 2. No slurs or hateful content

Slurs and hateful language are not allowed, regardless of intent, context, or whether you consider the word “reclaimed.”

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Images
- Videos
- Audio
- Usernames
- Nicknames
- Profile pictures
- Bios
- Memes
- Copypastas

## 3. NSFW content

NSFW content is prohibited **EXCEPT**:

- Light to moderate **_FICTIONAL_** gore may be allowed in appropriate spaces.
- Graphic **_FICTIONAL_** gore must be put under a spoiler with a clear warning.
- If you think a post may need approval, ask a moderator first by opening a ticket in the #tickets Discord channel.

All real-life NSFW content is prohibited, including, but not limited to:

- Nudity
- Sexual content
- Gore

## 4. No spam or disruptive behavior

Do not spam messages, emojis, reactions, images, links, copypastas, sound alerts/effects, or repeated characters.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Flooding chat
- Repeating the same message
- Excessive caps (some is okay, but don't do it too much)
- Purposely derailing conversations
- Attention-seeking behavior
- Baiting reactions from others
- Typing out lyrics to songs in any fashion (a few lines in a reference to a song is okay)

If you **_MUST_** scratch that spamming itch, please make a forum post in #the-endless-pit and do it there.

## 5. Raiding and trolling

Mentions of raiding this or any other community will result in disciplinary action. Trolling in this community will result in disciplinary action.

## 6. No self-promotion or unsolicited advertising

Do not advertise your own content, server, stream, social media, commissions, products, or communities without prior permission.

This includes:

- DM advertising
- Posting invite links
- "Check out my stream/server/channel"
- Promotion hidden inside unrelated conversations

This does not include:

- Sharing content you uploaded to YouTube or other platforms with the intent of sharing it as a reference, meme, or clip. Do not share content as a means of promoting your own content.
- Sharing product recommendations in related conversations (If a link is an affiliate link, you must clearly disclose that it is an affiliate link. Undisclosed affiliate links will be treated as a violation of this rule.)

## 7. No inappropriate or unsafe topics

Do **_NOT_** post or encourage:

- Sexual content
- Exploitative content
- Shock content
- Illegal content
- Encouragement of self-harm
- Encouragement of suicide
- Dangerous misinformation
- Real-life threats
- Doxxing

If a topic creates a safety issue or puts members at risk, it will be removed and possibly reported to the platform it takes place on.

## 8. No Religious/Political Talk

This community is not a place for political or religious debate. Keep all discussions based on these two topics and relating topics out of this community.

## 9. Respect Privacy

Do not share private or personal information about yourself or others without their permission. Remember, this is a public place on the internet, if you don't want it to be on the internet forever, don't post it.

This includes:

- Real names without permission
- Addresses
- Phone numbers
- Email addresses
- School/work information
- Private messages
- Doxxing or threats of doxxing

## 10. Follow platform ToS and Community Guidelines

### Discord

- https://discord.com/guidelines
- https://discord.com/terms

### Twitch

- https://safety.twitch.tv/s/article/Community-Guidelines
- https://legal.twitch.com/legal/terms-of-service/

## 11. No evading moderation

Do not attempt to avoid moderation actions.

This includes:

- Ban evasion
- Mute evasion
- Using alt accounts to get around punishment
- Having someone post on your behalf after being muted or banned

## 12. AI-generated content

AI-generated content is allowed as long as it is clearly disclosed as such and follows the rules in this document.

# Discord Specific Rules

## Channels

Use channels for their intended purpose. Off-topic posting, misuse of channels, or ignoring channel-specific rules may result in moderator action. (The #the-endless-pit is a great place to put off-topic posts)

## Spoilers and sensitive content

If you post spoilers for games, shows, movies, or major story content, use spoiler tags when appropriate.

Hints that are “basically spoilers” still count as spoilers.

Spoilers may be posted openly when:

- When a show, video, or movie has been out for over 1 year (365 calendar days), this does **_NOT_** apply to video games, books, or content I have said I have not seen but want to watch.
- When a web series episode has over 20 million views on a free public platform (like YouTube) **_AND_** has been out for over 1 month (30 calendar days) **_OR_** has been out for over 1 year (365 calendar days). (eg. The Amazing Digital Circus Ep 9 on July 19th 2026 as it will meet both 20 million views and one month, smaller shows may have to wait a full year to be allowed)

Sensitive content that is not technically NSFW but may disturb others should also be spoilered and labeled.

## Direct messages

Do not send unwanted DMs to community members, especially for:

- Advertising
- Harassment
- Sexual content
- Repeated bothering after being told to stop

## User profiles

Your username, display name, bio, status, and profile picture must also follow the rules.

## Alt accounts must be disclosed

One alt account may be allowed in the server, but it must be disclosed to a moderator when joining.

The alt account must also clearly identify its owner in its bio or profile where possible.

Undisclosed alt accounts may be removed, and the main account may also face moderation action.

If someone breaks the rules, do not argue with them in chat, let a moderator handle it, or report it in the #tickets channel on Discord.

## Respect stream flow

Do not demand games, attention, follows, mod, shoutouts, or specific reactions from me.

Do not repeatedly bring up topics after being asked to stop.

This includes asking to play with me in a game/stream where it has not been specifically announced and advertised as a viewers game/stream.

Use stream interaction features only when appropriate and do not abuse them.

Do not spam HudFX or similar features in a way that clutters the stream, distracts from content, or disrupts chat.

This includes paid HudFX effects, as much as I appreciate the donations, they can get disruptive and make making content with the stream harder.

## Viewers games

I know that some streamers allow viewers to play on their streams, but I do not allow viewers to join the games I am playing.

If someone is playing with me, we arranged that privately ahead of time.

If you are not in my lobby, do not try to join. This includes, but is not limited to:

- Joining without permission
- Repeatedly asking to join
- Contacting me to join during the stream

There are a few exceptions. If you have not been specifically told that you are one of them, then you are **_NOT_** an exception.

# Please remember

Rules function AS INTENDED. Just because you bend the wording of a rule into a pretzel and don't break the specific RAW (Rule As Written) does not mean you didn't break the RAI (Rule As Intended). Rules can be subject to interpretation and if the moderators and admins believe you broke a rule as it is INTENDED to function, you can expect the same consequences. All rules can apply to profiles as well as communications.

Moderators and admins may take action on behavior that is harmful, disruptive, or unsafe, even if it is not explicitly listed word-for-word in these rules.

These rules may change at any time, with or without warning. It is your job to keep up to date on the rules.

# Venting and Support

Everyone has tough times. Be it a bad day you wish to rant about, or an issue that has been with you for a while you wish to get support on, then there are many resources that can help.

I would like to state that while this community will allow light venting and ranting about light issues, no one here is a professional.

We cannot help you with major issues. We can offer support and a shoulder to lean on, but please realize we are just other people stuck behind a screen.

So, while this community can offer support for those who have friends and such here and wish to rant with a shoulder to lean on and people to listen, please remember that, once again, no one here is a professional and that there are other resources available.

If you are having a hard time in life, be it traumatic, mental health related, etc, please try and seek professional help!

If you are having any thoughts of _suicide_ or _self-harm_, PLEASE CONTACT YOUR LOCAL HOTLINE!

If you do not know your local hotline, [GOOGLE "_suicide hotline_" (or click here)](google.com/search?q=suicide+hotline) AND YOU WILL BE IMMEDIATELY TOLD.

`/m` = metaphor / metaphorically  
`/li` = literal / literally
